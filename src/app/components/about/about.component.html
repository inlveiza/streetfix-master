About StreetFix

Welcome to StreetFix

Your voice matters. StreetFix empowers you to report issues in your community quickly and easily, helping local authorities take action faster. Together, let's make our streets better for everyone.

StreetFix is a civic engagement platform that empowers communities to collaborate with local authorities in maintaining public infrastructure. Our system provides a direct channel for citizens to report issues while enabling municipalities to efficiently track and resolve concerns.

By bridging the gap between residents and government services, StreetFix fosters transparent communication and accountability, transforming community observations into actionable improvements for better public spaces.
