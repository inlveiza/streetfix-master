Welcome to StreetFix

Many community issues go unreported due to lack of an efficient system. StreetFix makes it easy to bring attention to problems that need fixing.

Report Dashboard

Displays all submitted reports with real-time status updates, allowing users and admins to track progress.

Upload Picture

Easily upload and attach photos to your reports to better illustrate community issues.

GPS Tracking

Automatically capture location data to accurately pinpoint reported issues on the map.

Community Upvoting

Vote on reports to highlight the most pressing issues in your community.
